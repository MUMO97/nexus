Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2025  ·  Effective immediately

This Privacy Policy describes how Nexus, developed by Murat Kolar, handles your data. The short version: Nexus does not collect, transmit, or store any of your personal data or environment data.

1. Data We Do Not Collect

Nexus does not collect telemetry, analytics, crash reports, usage data, or any personally identifiable information. No data from your Jamf Pro environment ever leaves your device or passes through any server controlled by the Developer.

2. Jamf Pro Credentials

Your Jamf Pro server URL, Client ID, and Client Secret are stored exclusively in the macOS Keychain on your device. They are never transmitted to the Developer or any third party. All API communication occurs directly between your Mac and your Jamf Pro server.

3. Local Storage

Nexus stores scan results and server profiles locally on your device using macOS UserDefaults and Keychain. This data never leaves your device except when communicating directly with your configured Jamf Pro server.

4. Subscriptions

If you purchase a Nexus Pro subscription, payment is processed by Paddle.com. The Developer receives only confirmation of your subscription status — no payment details, card numbers, or billing information. Paddle's privacy policy governs how they handle your payment data.

5. Updates

Nexus does not perform automatic update checks or phone home in any way. Updates are distributed manually via GitHub Releases.

6. Third-Party Services

Nexus has no third-party SDKs, analytics frameworks, advertising networks, or tracking libraries embedded in it.

7. Children's Privacy

Nexus is a professional IT administration tool not intended for use by anyone under 18. We do not knowingly collect data from minors.

8. Changes to This Policy

Any changes to this Privacy Policy will be posted on this page with an updated date. Continued use of the Software constitutes acceptance.

9. Contact

Privacy questions can be directed via GitHub Issues or the #nexus-dependency-analyzer channel on Mac Admins Slack.
